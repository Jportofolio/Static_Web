AIR FREIGHT & MARITIME FREIGHT

Atlantic Leader Link assists you with all your freights whether on air freight or maritime freight that cost you less to forward your packages from a city to another between South Africa and DR Congo. The advantage of the air freight is the fast, and time saving. Although, you can choose the maritime freight which help you send goods in heavy weight. Air freight service takes 5 days of shipping while the maritime freight may take you approximately 20 days for your products to be shipped to your destination.

IMPORT/EXPORT

With Atlantic Leader Link, there is no frontier for you to Import and Export your Good as much as you can to increase your streamline in the market as a business. Atlantic Leader link will require some tangible documents proving the purchase of your items before executing transaction of your goods. This is to comply with South African and DR Congo compliance in customs matters. We further notice that illegal items are not allowed .. contact your nearest branch to know further about your Export and import terms and conditions.
